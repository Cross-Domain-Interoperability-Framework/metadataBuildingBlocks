DataDescriptionWithStructure::Classes::AbstractDataStructureComponent

Definition

Abstract supertype for the cdi:DataStructureComponent family. Role given to a represented variable in the context of a data structure. Concrete subtypes: IdentifierComponent / MeasureComponent / AttributeComponent / DimensionComponent / VariableDescriptorComponent / VariableValueComponent. Full attribute structure (cdif:isDefinedBy_RepresentedVariable, cdi:has_PrimaryKey, etc.) lives in cdifDataType/cdifDataStructureComponent.

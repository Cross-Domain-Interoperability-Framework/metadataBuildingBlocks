DataDescriptionWithStructure::Classes::AbstractDataStructure

Definition

Abstract supertype for the four DataStructure variants. cdi:isStructuredBy on a DataDownload accepts any concrete subtype: DataStructure (general), DimensionalDataStructure (cube), LongDataStructure (variable-value-pair rows), WideDataStructure (wide-format tables).

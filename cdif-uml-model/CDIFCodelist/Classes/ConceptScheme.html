ConceptScheme

CDIFCodelist::Classes::ConceptScheme

Definition

A SKOS Concept Scheme - an aggregation of one or more SKOS Concepts, forming a controlled vocabulary or codelist. CDIF requires a resolvable @id, schema:identifier, schema:dateModified, and skos:hasTopConcept, plus either schema:license or schema:conditionsOfAccess.

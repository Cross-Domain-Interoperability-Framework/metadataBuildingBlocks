DDI-CDI InstanceVariable describes the use of a represented variable within a particular dataset, binding the conceptual variable to its physical realization. The root `cdi:InstanceVariable` carries `cdi:has_PhysicalSegmentLayout` (linking to the `ddicdiPhysicalSegmentLayout` BB), `cdi:has_ValueMapping` (a local `$defs/ValueMapping` capturing format, length, decimal handling, null sequences, and the W3C tabular-data-model parameters), and physical-level properties `cdi:physicalDataType`, `cdi:platformType`, and `cdi:source`.

It also carries the conceptual-level properties shared with `ddicdiRepresentedVariable`: `cdi:takesSubstantiveValuesFrom` and `cdi:takesSentinelValuesFrom` (referencing `ddicdiValueDomain`), `cdi:measures` (a local `UnitType`), `cdi:describedUnitOfMeasure`, `cdi:hasIntendedDataType`, `cdi:variableFunction`, and the substantive/sentinel conceptual-domain links. Local `$defs` cover `DataPoint`, `ConceptSystem`, `SubstantiveConceptualDomain`, `SentinelConceptualDomain`, `UnitType`, `ValueAndConceptDescription`, and `ValueMapping`. InstanceVariable is the variable-as-realized counterpart to `ddicdiRepresentedVariable` in the CDIF Data Description profile.
